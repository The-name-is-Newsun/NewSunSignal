Who We Are?

New Sun Signals is a pioneering initiative in its pre-startup stage, founded by Naveensurya and Gourab Ghosh, both students pursuing their BSc at Chennai Mathematical Institute. We are seeking pre-seed funding to establish a high-quality signal service, catering to retail traders and long-term investors.

What We Do?

At New Sun Trading Signals, our primary focus is providing accurate and timely signals for stocks, futures, options, commodities, and indices in the Indian Stock Market. Employing advanced algorithms and comprehensive market research, our team generates signals that enable well-informed decisions, capitalize on opportunities, and mitigate risks.

Our Approach

Recognizing the pivotal role of informed trading decisions, our approach emphasizes precision, reliability, and transparency. We are committed to delivering straightforward, easily comprehensible signals tailored for both novice and experienced traders.
